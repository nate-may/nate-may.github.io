Cycle The Continental Divide

Advisory: Only the Strongest Riders

The “Great” Continental Divide actually stretches from Alaska to the Caribbean. However, we max out our annual trip at 26.8 miles. Although we are based in Bozeman, Montana, once a year we take qualified groups over to Missoula for our annual Cycle the Continental Divide mountain-biking experience. This trip takes betweeen 5 to 8 hours, and tours the highest peaks in the Rattlesnake National Recreation Area. Select the trail type: Cycle the Continental Divide if you think you can handle it. Admission is $250 per participant. Price includes necessity pack, and the participants are accompanied by Mountain Spoke Senior Mountain Guides.
All participants must have proof of recent physical within the current year. Trip may result in dehydration, extreme fatigue, and an excessive dose of AWESOME! Completed safety waivers required at least 7 days prior to event in accordance with insurance policy. Dates and times may vary due to participant endurance levels, health, and weather conditions. Contact The Mountain Spoke for refund information. The Mountain Spoke does not take responsibility for physical injury that may occur any time during tour.
